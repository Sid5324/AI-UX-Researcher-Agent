# Data Agent Findings

The onboarding process has a high drop-off rate at the OAuth step, with only 28% of users completing this step, resulting in a 72% loss of potential users | The net promoter score has declined by 20% and user engagement has decreased by 7.9%, indicating a significant issue with the current onboarding experience

## Insights
- Users complain about too many steps in the onboarding process
- Major pain point: difficulty with OAuth integration
- 40% of users who drop off at OAuth step cite 'too much information required' as the reason
