### Problem Statement
The onboarding process has a high drop-off rate at the OAuth step, resulting in a 72% loss of potential users and a significant decline in net promoter score and user engagement

### Strategic Direction
Streamline the onboarding process by reducing the number of steps and simplifying the OAuth integration, potentially by using social media or other authentication methods
